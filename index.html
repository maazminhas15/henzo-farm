Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Grown with Passion

"Every bird is raised with integrity, every egg carries the warmth of our pasture."

Founded by Maaz Mustafa Minhas in the pristine foothills of Pindorian Shareef, Henzo Farm was born from a simple mission: to bring back the tradition of pure, heritage poultry breeding in Islamabad.

We specialize in three world-class heritage breeds — the record-breaking Black Australorp, the resilient Rhode Island Red, and the beloved Misri. From day-old chicks to mature laying hens, breeding pairs to farm-fresh organic eggs, we deliver quality you can taste and trust.
