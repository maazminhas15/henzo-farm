Common Questions

How is organic integrity maintained?
Our birds are fed on a strictly controlled diet of local grains and fresh organic greens grown on our own Islamabad pasture. We never use synthetic growth hormones, preventative antibiotics, or industrial cages.
Where is the sanctuary located?
Our premium sheds are located in Pindorian Shareef, Islamabad. This location was chosen for its clean air and pristine soil conditions. Visitation is strictly by pre-booked appointment only.
