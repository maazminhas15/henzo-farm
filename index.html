Our Story

Where Heritage
Meets Excellence

Founded by Maaz Mustafa Minhas, Henzo Farm was born from a vision to restore the tradition of pure heritage poultry breeding in the heart of Islamabad.

Nestled in the pristine foothills of Pindorian Shareef, our farm specializes in three world-class heritage breeds — the record-breaking Black Australorp, the resilient Rhode Island Red, and the beloved Misri.

Our Farm
Process

Transparency is our policy. Every step is designed with care and precision.

5:00 AM

Morning Harvest

Every day begins before sunrise. Our team collects fresh eggs from naturally nesting hens.

7:00 AM

Organic Feeding

Birds are fed organic grains, fresh greens, and clean water. No chemicals, no hormones.

9:00 AM

Health Check

Each bird undergoes daily wellness inspection by our veterinary partner.

10:00 AM

Quality Grading

Eggs are graded by size, shell quality, and yolk color. Only the finest make it.

11:00 AM

Careful Packing

Each egg is individually cushioned in biodegradable packaging.

12:00 PM

Same-Day Delivery

Orders dispatched within hours. Covers all of Islamabad and Rawalpindi.

Customer Follow-Up
