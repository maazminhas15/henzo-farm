About Henzo Farm

Henzo Farm was founded by Maaz Mustafa Minhas with a simple mission: to bring back the tradition of pure, heritage poultry breeding in Islamabad.

Located in the pristine foothills of Pindorian Shareef, our farm specializes in two world-class heritage breeds — the record-breaking Black Australorp and the resilient Rhode Island Red.

From day-old chicks to mature laying hens, from breeding pairs to farm-fresh organic eggs, we deliver quality you can taste and trust. Every egg has a golden yolk. Every bird is raised with integrity.

"Healthy Birds. Healthy Life."

Why Choose Henzo Farm?

We are Islamabad's trusted source for premium heritage poultry and organic eggs. Every bird is raised with care, fed organic grain, and given open pasture access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver across Islamabad & Rawalpindi?

Yes! We deliver to all sectors of Islamabad, Bahria Town, DHA, Gulberg Greens, PWD, and all areas of Rawalpindi including Saddar, Satellite Town, Adiala Road, and Westridge. Free delivery on orders over Rs 1,999.

Are your chickens vaccinated?

Absolutely. All our chicks receive standard vaccinations (Newcastle, Infectious Bronchitis, Gumboro). We provide vaccination records with every purchase. Our mature hens and roosters are also health-checked before delivery.

What makes your eggs different from store-bought?

Our hens are pasture-raised on organic feed with no chemicals or hormones. You will notice the deep golden yolk, richer flavor, and firmer whites. Many customers say our eggs taste like "eggs used to taste" — real farm freshness.

Can I visit the farm before buying?

Yes! We welcome farm visits at Pindorian Shareef, Islamabad. Please call or WhatsApp us at +92 311 5190548 to schedule a visit. Best times are Saturday and Sunday mornings between 8 AM and 12 PM.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and bank transfer. For large orders (breeding pairs), we require 50% advance payment to reserve your birds.
