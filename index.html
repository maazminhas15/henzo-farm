Common Enquiries

How do you ensure organic integrity?

Our birds are fed on a strictly controlled diet of local grains and fresh organic greens grown on our own Islamabad pasture. We never use synthetic growth hormones or industrial cages.

Do you provide delivery across Pakistan?

We currently specialize in Islamabad deliveries and farm pickups at Pindorian Shareef. Long-distance transport for live birds is arranged only through specialized temperature-controlled logistics to ensure bird safety.
